About Us

Built on the belief that every morning should start with a good cup of coffee, we strive to perfect every cup for you. Whether you have a busy and bustling day ahead or or are looking to have a slow start to your day, we are here for you!

We are constantly looking to bring together the local Baltimore community in more ways than one. Stop in and build lifelong connections with one of our many events held weekly for you and friends to enjoy.

Open six days a week, come and settle in to enjoy your favorite beverage and pastries. We'll save you a seat!
